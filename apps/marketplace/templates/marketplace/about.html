Our Mission

KasuMarketplace is the leading multi-vendor marketplace serving Kaduna State University (KASU). Our platform connects student buyers and sellers in a safe, secure campus-focused environment. Whether you're looking for gadgets, fashion, academic materials, or everyday essentials, KasuMarketplace makes buying and selling within KASU simple and reliable.
