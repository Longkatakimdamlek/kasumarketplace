🎓 About Us

Empowering Students Through Campus Commerce

KasuMarketplace is a modern online marketplace built specifically for students of Kaduna State University (KASU). Our goal is simple — to create a safe and trusted platform where students can buy, sell, and grow their businesses within the campus community.

🛍️

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students of Kaduna State University by giving them a digital platform to trade, earn, and connect. KasuMarketplace simplifies buying and selling on campus by bringing vendors and buyers together in one secure marketplace. From gadgets and fashion to academic materials and daily essentials, students can easily find what they need while supporting fellow students who run businesses within the university community.

Why KasuMarketplace?

Student Vendors

Students can easily open their own online store and start selling to fellow students on campus. KasuMarketplace encourages entrepreneurship and student businesses.

Secure Transactions

We are building strong verification and secure payment systems to ensure every buyer and seller can trade confidently on the platform.

Built for Campus Life

Designed specifically for campus needs — fast delivery, easy meetups, and convenient transactions within the university environment.
